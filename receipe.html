This is how to make pancake wiht a banana and it is so soft that you can have with honey or nutella.

Ingredient list

  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • butter

Steps to take

  1. Wisk eggs
  2. Mash banana
  3. Add eggs to banana
  4. Wisk to combine
  5. Add 1 tsp of butter and cook 1 min each side
  6. Then you can add some honey on top and enjoy it...

    7. This is after you finish above steps with a delicious look.
